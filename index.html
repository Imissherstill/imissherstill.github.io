Daily 9:30pm reminder

iOS won't let a web app fire a scheduled notification on its own. The reliable way is a built-in Shortcuts automation — 30 seconds to set up, works forever:
  1. Open the Shortcuts app → Automation tab
  2. Tap + → Time of Day → set 9:30 PM, Daily
  3. Choose Run Immediately
  4. Add action Open App → pick Momentum (or Safari to this page)
Done. Every night at 9:30 it'll pop the app open so you log your screen time and lock in the day.
